# GymTrack

A full-stack fitness tracking web app built for people who actually train.

## Live App
👉 [gym-tracker-web--hvx20.replit.app](https://gym-tracker-web--hvx20.replit.app)

## What it does

**Workout Templates**
Create and save your workout days (Push, Pull, Legs, Upper, Lower etc). Each template holds your exercises with target sets, reps, and weight. Hit Start when you're ready to train.

**Active Session Logging**
A live timer starts when you begin. Log your actual sets, reps, and weight as you go. Rest timer counts down automatically between sets. Finish and everything saves.

**Progress Tracking**
See how your strength has improved over time. The dashboard shows line charts for your most logged exercises — watch your numbers go up.

**Calorie Calculator**
Enter your stats and get your maintenance calories calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula. See your targets for aggressive cut, small cut, maintain, normal bulk, and aggressive bulk.

**Gym IQ**
29 AI-powered articles across 5 categories — Nutrition, Training, Supplements, Recovery, and Myths. Each article has a Simple mode (plain language, no jargon) and a Detailed mode (actual science and mechanisms). Powered by the Claude API.
